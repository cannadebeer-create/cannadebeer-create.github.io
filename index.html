FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an order take?
+
Most orders are completed within 5–7 business days from confirmation. Rush orders may be available — contact us to discuss your timeline.
Do you deliver nationwide?
+
Yes! We offer nationwide delivery across South Africa. You can also collect from our workshop in Johannesburg. Delivery costs depend on your location.
Can I fully customise my cover?
+
Absolutely. You can customise shell colour, trim, lining, numbering, patterns, and even add sports badges or logos. The builder shows some options — contact us for full custom requests.
What materials do you use?
+
We use premium synthetic leather for the outer shell — weather-resistant, soft-touch, and durable. The inner lining is soft fleece to protect your clubhead.
Do you do bulk / club orders?
+
Yes! We offer pricing for golf clubs, corporate gifts, and team orders. Get in touch with your requirements and we'll provide a custom quote.
How do I place an order?
+
Click any cover, choose "Build Your Own", design your cover, then send your order via WhatsApp. We'll confirm details and pricing before proceeding.
